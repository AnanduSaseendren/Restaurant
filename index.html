Our Chefs

Dominique Crenn

The first female chef in the U.S. to earn three Michelin stars, Dominique Crenn is known for her artistic, storytelling approach to French cuisine at Atelier Crenn in San Francisco.

Gordon Ramsay

Gordon Ramsay, a world-renowned Michelin-starred chef, is known for his fine dining expertise, fiery personality, and global restaurant empire.

Massimo Bottura

An Italian chef famous for blending tradition with innovation, Massimo Bottura is the mastermind behind Osteria Francescana, a three-Michelin-star restaurant.

Clare Smyth

A Michelin-starred British chef, Clare Smyth is renowned for her modern British cuisine at Core by Clare Smyth.
